Meet Our Founder

Advocate Aashish Kumar

Founder & CEO

"The power to transform society lies in the hands of our youth. Through legal awareness and social empowerment, we can create lasting change in our communities."

Advocate Aashish Kumar founded YSS in 2024, combining his legal expertise with a passion for youth empowerment. As a professional advocate practicing in Ramnagar District Court, he brings valuable legal knowledge to guide young minds in understanding their rights and responsibilities. His unique approach of integrating legal awareness with social activism has helped create meaningful impact in the community.
